What is Tortilla Fitmess?

A library of workouts inspired by people who want to workout from anywhere with minimal equipment. Start slow, select a few muscle groups to workout and use low or no weight if you are a beginner. *The creator of this website is not a certified fitness trainer hence ~*fit-mess*~

Choosing what to train?

The point is to train your enitre body. A balanced workout routine will work out most of your muscles by the end of the week. Working out your chest helps with posture, working out your back helps with back pain, working out your entire leg helps with knee pain. Do some research!

Train anywhere

Workouts that use minimal equipment mixed in with some high-heart rate workouts to give you plently of ideas. Was your workout too easy? Mix in heavier weights/more high-heart rate moves.

Freedom to do you

No expectations, no pay wall plans and no pressure. Move and learn at your own pace, not everyone needs to have fitstagram modal goals.
